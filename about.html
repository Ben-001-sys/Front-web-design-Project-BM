My Background Story

My journey into the digital world began with curiosity — a curiosity to understand how ideas become visuals, how visuals become experiences, and how experiences shape the world around us.

Over the years, I’ve grown through learning, experimentation, and real-world collaboration. Everything I create today reflects the story of becoming a better designer, developer, and storyteller.
